Peafowl Genetics Calculator

This calculator may be used to predict and visualize the offspring of any peafowl breeding pair, and to create and visualize the possible genetic makeup of any peafowl.

For more genetics information, please visit the main Peafowl Genetics page. For more information about this calculator (including answers to common questions), please visit the Calculator Home page.

Enter phenotype (visual traits) for a breeding pair of peafowl.

Note: Phenotypes resulting from multiple mutations are assumed homozygous, and will breed true. Enter hets/splits in the genotype section.
